Overview

EviSearch is a human-in-the-loop, multi-agent system for extracting ontology-aligned evidence tables directly from clinical trial PDFs. It combines document-aware querying, retrieval-guided evidence search, reconciliation across agents, and auditable provenance so each extracted value can be reviewed against the source document.

What The System Does

The pipeline parses clinical trial publications, runs parallel extraction agents over the document, resolves disagreements through verification, and presents the resulting evidence table through a review interface for auditing and feedback.

Why It Matters

Systematic reviews require reliable evidence extraction across narrative text, tables, and figures. EviSearch is designed to reduce manual burden while preserving traceability for high-stakes clinical evidence workflows.
